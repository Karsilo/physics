## Derivation

Proof $g$ is directly proportional to $r$ below the surface

using 

$$
\rho = \dfrac{m}{V}, \quad \quad V = \dfrac{4}{3}\pi r^2, \quad \quad g = \dfrac{GM}{r^2}
$$

we can solve for $m$

$$
m = \dfrac{4}{3}\pi r^3 \rho
$$

subbing $m$ into $g$

$$
g = \dfrac{4}{3}G \pi \rho r
$$

since 

$$
\dfrac{4}{3}G \pi \rho = \text{constant}
$$

then

$$
g \propto r
$$
