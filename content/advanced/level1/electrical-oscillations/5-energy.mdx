---
title: 5. Energy
description: -----
---

#### Energy on Inductor

$$
E_{mag} = \dfrac{1}{2}Li^2
$$

#### Energy on Capacitor

$$
E_{elec} = \dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{q^2}{C}
$$

#### Total Energy

$$
\dfrac{1}{2}Li^2 + \dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{q^2}{C} = \dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{Q^2}{C}
$$

or

$$
\dfrac{1}{2}Li^2 + \dfrac{1}{2}\dfrac{q^2}{C} = \dfrac{1}{2}LI^2
$$

#### Current

$$
i = \pm \sqrt{\dfrac{Q^2 - q^2}{LC}}
$$

#### Charge

$$
q = \pm \sqrt{Q^2 - \dfrac{LC}{i^2}}
$$
